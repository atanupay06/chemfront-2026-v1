About CHEMFRONT-2025

CHEMFRONT‑2025 is a two‑day international conference organized by the Department of Chemistry, Prabhat Kumar College, Contai, as part of the centenary celebration of the institution. It aims to bring together researchers to discuss emerging directions in chemistry at the interfaces of biology, physics, and advanced materials.

About the Institution

Established in 1926, Prabhat Kumar College, the oldest and one of the most reputed institutions in Purba Medinipur, West Bengal, proudly celebrates 100 years of academic excellence. The college offers UG and PG programmes in Arts, Commerce, and Science, with 24 Honours courses, 5 postgraduate programmes, and 20+ short-term skill-based courses. It also hosts two recognized research centres in Natural Sciences and Social Sciences & Humanities, affiliated with Vidyasagar University, offering Ph.D. programmes. Through NSS, UBA, and Science Forum units, the institution actively serves the community. Recognized with CPE status and funded under DST-FIST, the college proudly commemorated its Centenary in 2025.

  • Metal‑based therapeutics & bioinorganic systems
